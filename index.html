My Process

How the Magic Happens

From initial sketch to finished puppet, every creation follows a journey of craftsmanship.

01

Design & Sketch

Every puppet begins as a pencil sketch, capturing character personality and movement possibilities.

02

Pattern & Cut

Patterns are carefully drafted and premium fabrics are selected and cut with precision.

03

Stitch & Assemble

Hand-stitching brings the puppet to life, with careful attention to durability and movement.

04

Paint & Detail

Faces are hand-painted with personality, and finishing touches like hair and accessories added.
